The Power of Giving

What you can do

Leading University today is a global leader among major research universities—and philanthropy plays a tremendous part in its success.

We are grateful for your interest in making a gift. Donors help LU become more accessible, discover new approaches to treating disease, and grow more interconnected and innovative.

There are many ways you can contribute to our University. From scholarships to sports teams, faculty research to student clubs, there’s a cause at LU for you.

Explore opportunities to support:

  • Students

  • Faculty & Research

  • The campus and it's buildings

  • Programs & Festival Sponsorship

  • Clubs, activities, and all the things that bring the university experience to life


Or get in touch, and we’ll help you discover the part of BU that speaks to you.

We’ll also advise you on the best way for you to give, including some you may not know about. You may have more to offer than you think!
